# Injecting Disinfectant & UV Light Inside the Body

- **April 23, 2020 press conference:** Suggested researching injection of disinfectant as COVID treatment
- **Exact quote:** "And then I see the disinfectant, where it knocks it out in a minute, one minute. And is there a way we can do something like that by injection inside or almost a cleaning?" [Source: White House briefing video transcript, April 23, 2020]
- Also suggested bringing UV light **"inside the body"** somehow
- Lysol, Clorox, and poison control centers issued **emergency warnings** not to ingest disinfectants [Source: Emergency statements, April 2020]
- Next day claimed he was **"being sarcastic"** - video clearly shows he was addressing medical experts seriously [Source: CNN fact-check, April 24, 2020]
- Disinfectant poisoning calls spiked dramatically after the briefing [Source: CDC poison control data]
