# The "Tim Apple" Incident

- **March 2019:** At American Workforce Policy Advisory Board meeting, introduced Apple CEO Tim Cook as **"Tim Apple"** [Source: Video footage, March 6, 2019]
- Later claimed he said "Tim Cook Apple" very quickly to save time and words [Source: Trump tweets]
- Video clearly shows he said "Tim Apple" [Source: Multiple video recordings]
