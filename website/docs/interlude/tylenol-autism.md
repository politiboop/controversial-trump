# Tylenol Causes Autism (September 2025... Then Oops)

- **September 2025:** Trump claimed taking Tylenol during pregnancy causes autism at a press conference
- Told Americans **"Don't take Tylenol"** and claimed it's "associated with a very increased risk of autism" [Source: Press conference, September 2025; NPR, PBS]
- Claimed communities without Tylenol have **"no autism"** while others have 1 in 12 boys with autism [Source: White House statement, September 2025]
- **Medical reality:** Decades of research have not found a causal link between acetaminophen (Tylenol) and autism [Source: NPR, Nature, PolitiFact, September 2025]
- Tylenol is the **only over-the-counter pain reliever approved** for pregnant women [Source: Medical experts, PBS NewsHour]
- Forgoing fever treatment can cause **maternal and fetal harm** [Source: NPR, September 2025]
- Trump quietly stopped mentioning this claim within weeks after medical backlash
