# "The Weave" Defense

- Trump calls his rambling, incoherent speeches **"the weave"** and claims it's a sign of **"a brilliant mind"** [Source: Multiple 2024 interviews; Axios, Washington Post]
- Described his speaking style: **"I weave stories"** to connect disparate topics [Source: Campaign statements, 2024]
- Language experts describe it as **"erratic"** and **"confusing"** [Source: Washington Post analysis, October 2024]
