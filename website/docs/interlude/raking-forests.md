# Raking Forests to Prevent Wildfires

- **November 2018:** After devastating California wildfires, suggested the problem was **lack of "raking"**
- **"You've got to take care of the floors. You know the floors of the forest, very important... they don't do any forest management"** [Source: Press conference, November 17, 2018]
- Claimed **Finland's president** told him they prevent fires by raking
- Finland's president denied ever saying this [Source: Finnish media interviews, November 2018]
- California wildfires driven by climate change, drought, and wind - not insufficient raking
