# Schoolchildren Getting Gender Surgery (2024)

- Claimed schools were giving children **gender surgery** without parental consent
- **"Your kid goes to school and comes home a few days later with an operation"** [Source: Campaign rally, 2024; U.S. News fact-check]
- Completely false - no schools perform surgeries [Source: Fact-checkers, 2024]
