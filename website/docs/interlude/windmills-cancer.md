# Windmills Cause Cancer

- **April 2019 rally:** "If you have a windmill anywhere near your house, congratulations, your house just went down 75 percent in value. And they say the noise causes cancer" [Source: Rally transcript, April 2, 2019]
- No scientific evidence supports this claim [Source: American Cancer Society, multiple studies]
- Repeated variations of this claim at multiple rallies
