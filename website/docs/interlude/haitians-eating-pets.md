# Haitians Eating Pets in Springfield, Ohio (2024)

- **September 2024 debate:** Trump claimed Haitian immigrants in Springfield, Ohio were **"eating the dogs"** and **"eating the cats"** [Source: Presidential debate, September 10, 2024]
- **"They're eating the pets of the people that live there"** [Source: Debate transcript]
- City officials, police, and local residents confirmed this was **completely false** [Source: Springfield city manager, police statements]
- JD Vance admitted he **"created"** the story to draw attention to immigration [Source: CNN interview, September 2024]
- **PolitiFact named it the 2024 "Lie of the Year"** - Trump's **fourth** such award [Source: PolitiFact, December 2024]
- Led to bomb threats against Springfield schools and hospitals [Source: News reports, September 2024]
- Trump never retracted the claim and repeated it at rallies
