- Turned a town hall Q&A into a **39-minute music listening session**
- Stopped taking questions and just played songs while swaying on stage [Source: Town hall event, Pennsylvania, October 2024; Axios]
- Crowd stood confused as Trump listened to his playlist [Source: News coverage, October 2024]
