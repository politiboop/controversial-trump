# Violent Rhetoric & Death Threats Against Political Opponents

Trump repeatedly used violent rhetoric and suggested political opponents deserved execution:

## "Hang Mike Pence" - January 6, 2021

- Mob erected **gallows outside Capitol** and chanted **"Hang Mike Pence!"** [Source: Reuters, PBS, video evidence]
- Trump tweeted attack on Pence **while mob was hunting him** in Capitol: "Mike Pence didn't have the courage to do what should have been done" [Source: Twitter archive, 2:24 PM EST, Jan 6, 2021]
- Secret Service rushed Pence to secure location; came within **40 feet** of mob [Source: Washington Post, Secret Service testimony to Jan 6 Committee]
- When told rioters were chanting "Hang Mike Pence," Cassidy Hutchinson testified under oath that Chief of Staff Mark Meadows told White House Counsel Pat Cipollone that Trump said **"Mike Pence deserves it"** or words to that effect [Source: House Jan 6 Committee hearing testimony, June 28, 2022; CNBC, Rolling Stone]
- Trump denied this account on Truth Social [Source: Trump Truth Social post, June 2022]
- Never condemned the death threats during the riot [Source: Review of Trump's public statements Jan 6]
- Later said Pence was **"mortally wounded"** politically for not overturning election [Source: Trump interview statements]

## General Mark Milley "Execution" Post (September 2023)

- Truth Social post: Milley's calls to China were **"an act so egregious that, in times gone by, the punishment would have been DEATH!"** [Source: Trump Truth Social, September 2023; NBC News]
- Suggested America's top general committed **treason** for reassuring allies after January 6 [Source: Atlantic, Washington Post]
- Milley subsequently received death threats; had to take security precautions [Source: Atlantic reporting, September 2023]
- Former officials called it **"witness intimidation"** ahead of potential testimony [Source: Legal experts quoted in CNN, NBC]

## Calls for Executing Political Opponents

- Repeatedly suggested Democrats committed **"treason"** - a capital offense [Source: Multiple Truth Social posts, 2022-2025]
- Shared posts calling for **"military tribunals"** for Democrats [Source: Truth Social archives, Media Matters documentation]
- Retweeted accounts calling for Obama, Clinton, Biden to face **"justice" for "treason"** [Source: Twitter/Truth Social archives]
- **"Enemy from within"** rhetoric - called Democrats bigger threat than Russia/China [Source: Fox News interview, October 2024]
- Said military should be used against **"radical left lunatics"** on Election Day [Source: Fox News interview, October 2024; ABC News]
- Suggested Liz Cheney should face **"nine barrels shooting at her"** (November 2024) [Source: Campaign rally Arizona, November 1, 2024; CNN, NBC News]

## Attacking Democrats for Defending Lawful Military Orders (November 2025)

- Six Democratic lawmakers (Sens. Elissa Slotkin, Mark Kelly; Reps. Jason Crow, Maggie Goodlander, Chrissy Houlahan, Chris Deluzio) posted video reminding military to refuse illegal orders [Source: NBC News, Washington Post, November 2025]
- Trump accused them of **"seditious behavior"** and called for them to be **"arrested and put on trial"** [Source: Trump Truth Social posts, NBC News, November 20, 2025]
- Trump: Sedition is **"punishable by death"** [Source: Trump Truth Social, November 2025]
- Reposted message saying **"Hang them George Washington would"** [Source: Al Jazeera, Washington Post, November 2025]
- Legal experts confirm the lawmakers were simply restating existing law [Source: FactCheck.org, PolitiFact, November 2025]
- Military law experts: Soldiers are **legally required** to refuse unlawful orders [Source: Uniform Code of Military Justice, Article 92; Florida International University law professor Eric R. Carpenter]
- Sen. Slotkin received hundreds of death threats, given 24/7 Capitol Police protection [Source: Sen. Slotkin statement, ABC News, November 2025]

## Promoting Violence Against Media

- Shared video of him **body-slamming CNN logo** [Source: Twitter post, July 2017; CNN, Washington Post]
- Praised congressman who body-slammed reporter: **"Any guy that can do a body slam... he's my kind of guy"** (referring to Rep. Greg Gianforte assaulting Guardian reporter Ben Jacobs) [Source: Montana rally, October 2018; CNN]
- Called press **"enemy of the people"** - phrase used by Stalin, Hitler, Mao [Source: Multiple tweets and rally speeches, 2017-2021]
- Laughed when supporter suggested **"shooting"** migrants at rally [Source: Florida Panhandle rally, May 2019; New York Times]
- Never condemned bomb threats against news organizations after his attacks [Source: Review of public statements following October 2018 mail bomb incidents]
