# Praise for Dictators and Authoritarians

Trump repeatedly praised, defended, and expressed admiration for the world's most brutal dictators:

## Vladimir Putin (Russia)

- Called Putin **"genius"** and **"savvy"** for invading Ukraine (February 2022) [Source: Clay Travis and Buck Sexton Show, February 22, 2022; CNN]
- **"I think he's done an amazing job of taking the mantle"** when Putin invaded Crimea (2014) [Source: MSNBC Morning Joe interview, March 2014]
- Sided with Putin over US intelligence agencies at Helsinki summit (2018) [Source: Press conference transcript, July 16, 2018; White House]
- Said he **"trusts"** Putin despite Russian election interference [Source: Multiple interviews, 2016-2018]

## Kim Jong Un (North Korea)

- **"We fell in love"** after exchanging letters (2018) [Source: West Virginia rally, September 29, 2018; Washington Post]
- Called their letters **"beautiful letters"** and **"love letters"** [Source: Multiple rally speeches, 2018-2019]
- **"He wrote me beautiful letters and they're great letters"** [Source: Interview statements, 2019]
- Praised Kim despite him executing his uncle and starving his people [Source: UN human rights reports, BBC documentation]

## Xi Jinping (China)

- Called Xi **"brilliant"** for making himself president for life [Source: CNN reporting, March 2018]
- **"He's now president for life... I think it's great. Maybe we'll have to give that a shot someday"** (2018) [Source: Closed-door Mar-a-Lago remarks, CNN, March 3, 2018]
- Praised Xi's COVID response while 1 million+ Uyghurs detained in camps [Source: Multiple 2020 interviews; UN estimates on Uyghur detention]

## Other Authoritarian Leaders Praised

- **Erdogan (Turkey):** "He does things the right way"—after crackdown on dissent [Source: White House meeting, May 2017; Washington Post]
- **Duterte (Philippines):** Praised his drug war that killed thousands extrajudicially [Source: Phone call transcript, April 2017; leaked intercept]
- **el-Sisi (Egypt):** "He's a fantastic guy"—despite torture and mass detention [Source: White House meeting, April 2017; CNN]
- **Orban (Hungary):** "He's a strong man... a smart man"—EU's most autocratic leader [Source: White House visit, May 2019; Reuters]
- **Bolsonaro (Brazil):** "He's a great president"—called "Trump of the Tropics" [Source: Multiple statements, 2019-2020; BBC]
