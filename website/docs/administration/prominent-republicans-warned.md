# Prominent Republicans Who Warned Against Trump

Beyond his own administration, numerous high-profile Republicans, senators, governors, and conservative leaders publicly warned Americans not to re-elect Trump:

## Mitt Romney - Senator & 2012 GOP Presidential Nominee

- **"I believe that Donald Trump was not successful as president and that his character and his conduct make him unfit to be president again"** [Source: CNN interview, 2023]
- Called Trump **"manifestly unfit for office"** after January 6 [Source: Senate floor speech, 2021]
- Only Republican senator to vote to convict Trump in **both impeachment trials**
- **"The president's conduct... was a flagrant assault on our electoral process and on our constitutional process"** [Source: Senate speech]

## Liz Cheney - Former House GOP Conference Chair

- **"Donald Trump is unfit for any office"** [Source: ABC News interview, 2022]
- **"He can never be trusted with power again"** after January 6 investigation
- Lost GOP primary for opposing Trump; called it **"the beginning of taking our party back"**
- Endorsed Kamala Harris: **"I know Donald Trump. I know how dangerous he is"** [Source: 2024 campaign event]
- **"No conservative can support Donald Trump"** [Source: Multiple interviews]

## Adam Kinzinger - Former House GOP Member

- **"Donald Trump should not be president again. He's shown he can't handle power"** [Source: CNN, 2022]
- Founded **"Country First"** movement to oppose Trump
- Endorsed Joe Biden: **"Donald Trump is a threat to democracy"**
- Called Trump **"morally bankrupt"** and **"fundamentally broken"**
- **"I'd rather lose an election than lose my soul"** [Source: Book "Renegade"]

## Chris Christie - Former Governor & 2024 Presidential Candidate

- **"Donald Trump is unfit to be president of the United States"** [Source: 2024 GOP primary debates]
- Called Trump **"a child"** who **"only cares about himself"**
- **"Someone who I trusted with the most powerful office in the world betrayed that trust"** after January 6
- Suspended 2024 campaign rather than risk helping Trump win nomination

## Asa Hutchinson - Former Arkansas Governor

- **"Donald Trump is morally disqualified from being president again"** [Source: 2023 interviews]
- Called on Trump to **"step aside"** after January 6
- **"We cannot have a president who puts himself above the Constitution"**

## Larry Hogan - Former Maryland Governor

- **"We need to move on from Donald Trump"** [Source: Multiple interviews, 2022-2024]
- **"He's a loser who has cost us election after election"**
- Refused to endorse Trump despite being GOP Senate nominee

## Geoff Duncan - Former Georgia Lt. Governor

- **"Donald Trump is a liability for our party and our country"** [Source: CNN, 2023]
- Testified against Trump in Georgia election interference case
- **"Country over party"** - endorsed no candidate rather than support Trump

## John Kasich - Former Ohio Governor & 2016 GOP Candidate

- **"I cannot and will not support Donald Trump"** [Source: 2020, 2024 statements]
- Spoke at 2020 Democratic National Convention against Trump
- **"The stakes are too high for our country"**

## The Lincoln Project & Conservative Leaders

- **Over 200 former Republican officials** signed letters opposing Trump [Source: Republicans for the Rule of Law, 2020, 2024]
- Conservative lawyers group: **"Donald Trump is a clear and present danger to American democracy"**
- **"Duty to Country"** - Group of former Trump officials opposing his return
