# His Own Cabinet Warned America Not to Re-Elect Him

An unprecedented number of Trump's own senior officials, cabinet members, and military leaders publicly warned Americans against re-electing him. **USA Today:** This level of criticism from former officials had **"no historical precedent in the last century."** **Washington Post:** "No president had ever drawn more detractors from inside his inner-circle." **PBS:** "Without precedent in the modern era." **CNN:** "No person in US politics has such an expansive list of high-profile allies turned enemies."

## John Kelly - White House Chief of Staff (2017-2019)

Four-star Marine general, Gold Star father (lost son in Afghanistan), longest-serving Trump Chief of Staff:

- **"A person that has no idea what America stands for and has no idea what America is all about"** [Source: CNN interview with Jake Tapper, October 2023]
- **"A person who admires autocrats and murderous dictators"** [Source: CNN interview, October 2023]
- **"A person that has nothing but contempt for our democratic institutions, our Constitution, and the rule of law"** [Source: CNN interview, October 2023]
- Asked about prospect of second Trump term: **"God help us"** [Source: New York Times interview, 2023]
- **[Disputed]** Confirmed Trump called fallen soldiers **"suckers" and "losers"** [Source: CNN confirmation, The Atlantic reporting, September 2020]
  - *Originally reported by anonymous sources in The Atlantic; John Kelly later confirmed; Trump denies making these statements*
- **[Disputed]** Confirmed Trump complained about military amputees: **"doesn't look good for me"** [Source: The Atlantic, 2020]
  - *Trump denies this account*
- **[Disputed]** Confirmed Trump said Hitler **"did some good things, too"** on multiple occasions [Source: CNN interview, The Atlantic, 2023]
  - *Kelly confirmed this account; Trump denies it*
- **Opinion:** Said Trump **"prefers the dictator approach to government"** [Source: New York Times interview, 2023]
- **Opinion:** Said Trump falls into **"general definition of fascist"** [Source: New York Times audio interview, October 2024]
- **[Disputed]** Confirmed Trump wanted **"German generals" like Hitler had"** [Source: The Atlantic, CNN confirmation]
  - *Trump denies this*

## Gen. Mark Milley - Chairman Joint Chiefs of Staff (2019-2023)

Four-star Army general, 40+ years of service, highest-ranking military officer in the nation:

- **Opinion:** Called Trump **"fascist to the core"** [Source: Bob Woodward's "War," 2024]
- **Opinion:** Called Trump **"the most dangerous person to this country"** [Source: Bob Woodward's "Peril," 2021]
- **Opinion:** Retirement speech: Trump was a **"wannabe dictator"** [Source: Retirement ceremony, September 29, 2023]
- **"We don't take an oath to an individual. We take an oath to the Constitution, and we take an oath to the idea that is America—and we're willing to die to protect it"** [Source: Retirement speech, September 2023]
- Told Woodward he feared being court-martialed if Trump won in 2024 [Source: "War" by Bob Woodward, 2024]
- Said he had "suspicions" about Trump's mental decline, eventually concluded **"he's a total fascist"** [Source: The Atlantic, Woodward books]
- **"Deeply convinced"** Trump remained a danger after losing 2020

## Gen. James "Mad Dog" Mattis - Secretary of Defense (2017-2019)

Retired four-star Marine general, revered military leader:

- Called Trump a **"threat to the Constitution"**
- **"Donald Trump is the first president in my lifetime who does not try to unite the American people — does not even pretend to try. Instead he tries to divide us"**
- **"We must reject and hold accountable those in office who would make a mockery of our Constitution"**
- Blamed Trump for **"inciting mob rule"** after January 6
- So worried Trump would order nuclear strike that he **slept in gym clothes** for emergency overnight calls
- Privately agreed with Milley that Trump was a threat to the country (Woodward book)
- Resignation letter criticized Trump for not "treating allies with respect" and affinity for dictators

## Mark Esper - Secretary of Defense (2019-2020)

- **"Trump is not fit for office because he puts himself first, and I think anybody running for office should put the country first"**
- Called Trump **"a threat to democracy"**
- Said Trump wanted U.S. troops to **shoot American protesters** during George Floyd protests
- Warned Trump might use military for political purposes in second term
- Explicitly said he would not vote for Trump
- Famously fired by Trump via Twitter

## John Bolton - National Security Adviser (2018-2019)

- **"A mountain of facts demonstrates that Trump is unfit to be president"**
- **"If his first four years were bad, a second four will be worse"**
- Said Trump **"too dumb to be a fascist"**
- Warned there would be **"celebrations in the Kremlin"** if Trump re-elected
- Said Trump has no coherent national security strategy beyond self-interest
- Wrote in 2020 and 2024 elections to avoid voting for Trump

## H.R. McMaster - National Security Adviser (2017-2018)

Three-star Army general, Gulf War hero:

- Described Oval Office meetings as **"exercises in competitive sycophancy"**
- Detailed outlandish Trump remarks: **"Why don't we just bomb the drugs?"** in Mexico
- Trump asked: **"Why don't we take out the whole North Korean Army during one of their parades?"**

## Other Senior Officials Who Warned Against Trump

- **Vice President Mike Pence:** "In good conscience" could not endorse Trump
- **Rex Tillerson (Secretary of State):** Said Trump **"totally ignorant"** of American history and world events
- **Dan Coats (Director of National Intelligence):** Resigned under pressure, opposed Trump's re-election
- **Olivia Troye (COVID advisor to Pence):** Trump didn't want to give aid to disaster victims in California because it was a blue state
- **Miles Taylor (DHS Chief of Staff):** Endorsed Harris, was anonymous "Anonymous" author warning about Trump
- **William Barr (Attorney General):** "Categorically opposed" to Kash Patel at FBI
- **Ty Cobb (White House Special Counsel):** Endorsed Kamala Harris
- **Alyssa Farah Griffin (Communications Director):** Endorsed Harris
- **ONLY HALF** of Trump's first-term cabinet endorsed his 2024 campaign
