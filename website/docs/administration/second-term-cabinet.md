# Unqualified & Dangerous Second-Term Cabinet

Trump's second-term cabinet prioritizes loyalty and television experience over qualifications. **The Guardian:** picks are **"more extreme than those proposed in 2016."** **Politico:** nominees are **"primarily motivated by loyalty."** **Reuters and AP:** "television experience seemed to be a trait that was prioritized." **Center for Science in the Public Interest:** "It's as if Donald Trump is poised to turn over control of our vital health and medical agencies to a clown car full of unqualified quacks."

## EJ Antoni - Bureau of Labor Statistics Nominee (WITHDRAWN)

Trump nominated a January 6 participant to oversee the nation's employment and inflation data, but withdrew the nomination after controversy:

- **EJ Antoni** - Heritage Foundation economist nominated to lead Bureau of Labor Statistics [Source: NBC News, August 2025]
- **Present at Capitol on January 6, 2021** - Video shows him on both west and east sides of Capitol grounds [Source: CNN KFile, August 2025]
- White House claimed he was merely a **"bystander"** who was "in town for meetings"
- Frequent guest on **Steve Bannon's "War Room"** podcast
- Previously suggested BLS should **stop publishing monthly job reports** - wanted quarterly reports instead
- Trump fired previous BLS head claiming she **"rigged" jobs reports**
- **CNN KFile investigation** revealed Antoni operated deleted Twitter account with sexually degrading attacks on Kamala Harris, derogatory remarks about gay people, and conspiracy theories [Source: CNN, August 2025]
- GOP Sens. Susan Collins and Lisa Murkowski declined to meet with Antoni
- **Nomination withdrawn September 2025** after mounting opposition [Source: NBC News, Axios, September 2025]
- Nomination raised concerns about politicizing economic statistics and appointing January 6 participants to key government positions

## Robert F. Kennedy Jr. - Secretary of Health and Human Services

Environmental lawyer with **no medical degree, no public health experience**. Oversees CDC, FDA, NIH, CMS, and 13 other health agencies:

- Named by Center for Countering Digital Hate as one of **"Disinformation Dozen"** - leading spreaders of COVID vaccine misinformation
- Falsely claimed COVID-19 was **"bioweapon"** made by China that was ethnically targeted
- Falsely claimed Ashkenazi Jews and Chinese were immune to COVID
- Promoted debunked theory linking vaccines to autism for decades
- Promised Sen. Bill Cassidy he would not change CDC vaccine statements - then **changed CDC website to suggest vaccines might cause autism**
- **Fired all 17 members of CDC vaccine advisory panel (ACIP)**
- Replaced them with anti-vaccine activists and conspiracy theorists
- New panel chair (Kirk Milhoan) falsely claimed COVID vaccines cause cancer, miscarriage, heart disease
- New panel voted to **remove hepatitis B vaccine recommendation for newborns**
- Fired CDC director Susan Monarez for refusing to promote misinformation
- Nine former CDC directors jointly condemned Kennedy: **"Endangering every American's health"**
- **Measles cases reached nearly 20-year high in 2025**; two unvaccinated children died in Texas
- **Pertussis (whooping cough) cases rising**; two unvaccinated children died in Kentucky
- **Article of impeachment filed** against Kennedy in December 2025
- His own sister Kerry Kennedy and cousin Caroline Kennedy expressed distrust
